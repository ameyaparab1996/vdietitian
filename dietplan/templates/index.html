How to get Diet plan?

Follow these 3 steps.
Step 1. Sign Up

Sign up by clicking on the signup button present on the right side of the header.

Step 2. Fill the form

After signing up, you will see a dropdown button on the right side of the header. Click on it and then click 'Fill out the form' button. Fill the form and click proceed.

Step 3. My Profile

Now, at the same place where you previously clicked 'Fill out form', now click on 'My profile' section. Here, you will see your Dietplan and other provided information.
